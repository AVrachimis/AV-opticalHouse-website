About Us

Tradition it seems, runs through this family like no other, since father Andreas launched his first shop in 1953, some sixty five years ago. He laid the foundation in optical services in Cyprus, having gained experience working with a chemist who also sold optics, so it was there he learnt the craft in optics. Now there’s six members of family continuing with his legacy
